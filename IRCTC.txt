ABOUT IRCTC

IRCTC (Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation) is the official online railway ticket booking platform of Indian Railways

Website: www.irctc.co.in

Customer Care: 14646, 080-44XX-7XXX

TICKET BOOKING

Login is required to book tickets

CANCELLATION & REFUND

Tickets can be cancelled online through IRCTC account

Refund rules:

>48 hours before departure: Full refund minus clerkage

48-12 hours: 25% deduction clerkage

12-4 hours: 50% deduction+ clerkage

<4 hours: No refund (except special cases)

TDR (Ticket Deposit Receipt) can be filed for:

Train cancellation

Train running late

AC failure

Coach not attached
